ALYSSA MEIER

Hello! This is me.

I'm a Peruvian researcher and information designer, currently based in Helsinki. Before pursuing an MA in Visual Communications Design at Aalto University, I worked for many years as a lawyer.

My current practice is varied, with a special interest in research, information design and data storytelling, particularly regarding law and policy matters.

Additionally, I am passionate about writing and sequential storytelling, and I have published short comics in several anthologies (see here).

This page is an archive of all my projects, personal and professional, including comics, writing, fine art, legal stuff, research, and design. Also, I made this website. I'm cool.
